What We Do

Complete Lawn & Tree Services

From weekly lawn maintenance to full tree removal and stump grinding — Levi and his crew handle it all so your Florida property looks its best year-round.

Full Lawn Maintenance

Regular mowing, edging, trimming, and clean-up. We keep your grass healthy and your property looking sharp all year long in Florida's growing climate.

Tree Trimming & Removal

Professional pruning and complete removal of palms, pines, oaks, and more. We keep canopies shaped and safely take down trees of any size.

Stump Grinding

Old stumps taking up yard space? We grind them down flush with the ground so you can reclaim your lawn and eliminate tripping hazards.

Sod Installation

Bare patches or a complete lawn refresh — we prep the soil and lay fresh sod for a lush, green result that withstands Florida's heat and rain.

Hedge Removal & Trimming

We shape overgrown hedges into clean, crisp lines or completely remove unwanted hedgerows to give your landscaping a fresh, open feel.

Yard Cleanup & Debris Removal

Storm cleanup, seasonal yard cleanouts, and debris hauling. We leave your property clean, safe, and ready to enjoy after any job.
